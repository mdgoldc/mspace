ABOUT US

Our Commitment to Your Workflow

At MSpace, we believe that your environment dictates your output. We started MSpace to solve the problem of the noisy modern open-plan office. We provide flexible, on-demand private spaces.

  • Soundproof Technology: Multi-layered acoustic panels for maximum isolation.
  • Air Quality: Constant airflow refresh systems to keep you alert.
  • Smart Access: Book instantly and access pods via our mobile app.

What You Get

Fast & Secure Wi-Fi

Blazing fast fiber-optic internet dedicated to each pod, ensuring seamless video calls and heavy uploads.

Ergonomic Furniture

Height-adjustable desks and premium lumbar-support chairs designed for long hours of comfortable work.

Charging Station

Every pod comes equipped with universal power outlets, USB-C fast charging, and wireless charging pads.

Smart Climate Control

Adjust the lighting temperature and airflow speed to suit your personal preference using the internal control panel.
